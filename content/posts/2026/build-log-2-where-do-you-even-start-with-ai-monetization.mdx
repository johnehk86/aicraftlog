## How Blog Monetization Actually Works

The model is straightforward:

### Step 1: Write Content

Create valuable content on your blog. Optimize for SEO (Search Engine Optimization) so Google ranks your posts on the first page when people search for relevant topics.

### Step 2: Traffic Comes In

Visitors find your blog through Google search, social media (Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit), YouTube, and developer communities.

### Step 3: Ads Generate Revenue

Google AdSense automatically displays relevant ads. When visitors see or click ads, you earn money. Typical rates: $0.01â€“$0.10 per click, or $1â€“$10 per 1,000 page views.

### Step 4: Reinvest and Grow
